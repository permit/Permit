Find a person by last name or Kerberos username

This web page allows you to look up a person at MIT based on his/her last name or Kerberos username. Only people with Kerberos names will be shown.

You need secure certificates to do this lookup. First, enter a last name or Kerberos username. Then click on the Submit button, and you will see a list of all matching people at MIT.
