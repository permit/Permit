Show Redundant Authorizations

This web page allows you to display a list of redundant Authorizations in the perMIT system if you have the appropriate viewing authorization for authorizations in the desired category. An authorization A is redundant if there exists an authorization B for the same person and function, with matching do_function and grant fields, where the qualifier in B is a superset (or parent) of the qualifier in A.

Option 1: Show redundant authorizations for a person
Enter the Kerberos username to whom the authorizations apply (not the person who made the changes). Then pick a category, and click the SUBMIT button.

Enter a qualifier_code (such as FC_SLOAN or 0HPC00005) for a branch of one of the qualifier hierarchies. Then pick a category, and click the SUBMIT button.
