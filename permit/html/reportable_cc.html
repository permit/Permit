Show Cost Objects on Which a User Can Report

To see the list of cost objects or funds on which a person can do reports in SAP, enter the person's username in the Kerberos username field and click on the Submit button.

Since reporting authorizations in the perMIT system can be defined by either Funds/Fund Centers or Cost Objects/Profit Centers, you may see reporting authorizations for objects from both the FUND and COST hierarchies.

(To see authorizations for users other than yourself, you need a special viewing authorization.)
