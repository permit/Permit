Qualifier types and their prefixes


Qualifier codes in the perMIT system often begin with an alphabetic prefix. Usually, when you are asked to enter a Qualifier Code, you must enter the whole qualifier code, including the alphabetic prefix. For example:

Type of objectQualifier typePrefix Example
Cost CenterCOST CC1644500
Internal OrderCOST II1030100
WBS (Project)COST PP1072000
Profit CenterCOST PCPC405300
Group of prof. ctrs.*COST 0HPC0HPC00102
FundFUND FF1644500
Fund Center (standard)FUND FCFC100033
Fund Center (custom)FUND FC_FC_VPIS
Spending GroupSPGP SG_SG_VPIS

* The 0HPC prefix starts with the numeral zero (0), not letter oh (O).
